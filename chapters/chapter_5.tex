\section{Experiment: Frequency Separation and Switching}
\label{sec:freq-separation}

\textit{Question: can the sensing pipeline separate two beacons by their modulation frequency and switch between them?}

With two lights present, the static Teensy setup records the SNR of both modulation frequencies at the same time and switches the tracked frequency from one light to the other, confirming that beacons can be distinguished and selected by frequency.

\section{Experiment: Sequential Two-Target Traverse}

\textit{Question: does the fused controller navigate to two beacons in sequence in an unobstructed real-world flight?}

The drone navigates to each beacon in turn on the CrazyFlie~2.1, validating that the full state machine sequences through multiple targets correctly on the real platform in open-space conditions.
