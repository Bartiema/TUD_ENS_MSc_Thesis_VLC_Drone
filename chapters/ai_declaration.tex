\chapter*{Declaration on the Use of Artificial Intelligence}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Declaration on the Use of Artificial Intelligence}

During the preparation of this thesis, the author made use of generative artificial-intelligence tools (large language models) to assist with language editing, rephrasing, and the structuring of text, as well as for occasional support while writing and debugging analysis and figure-generation code. All such output was reviewed, verified, and edited by the author, who takes full responsibility for the content of this thesis. The research itself---the hardware design, the controller, the experiments, and their interpretation---was conceived and carried out by the author. No generative tool was used to produce experimental data or results.
